Tracking

This website does not make use of cookies or complicated tracking software.

To have a basic view of how many times articles are seen, an (empty) image on the subdomain track.baobob.org is accessed on each page. Telling your browser to deny accesses to track.baobob.org will not alter the visual of this website if you decide to remove those images.
